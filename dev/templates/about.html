A Whole Latte Love

Our Mission

Muir Woods strives to provide a positive experience and quality product. We carry fair trade coffee from an award winning local roaster, organic loose leaf teas, and a selection of pastries from a local bakery.

We work to embody individuals from all backgrounds, interests, and places in life and create the opportunity to make unexpected connections and friendships.

 We are a space run by students for other students and we hope to foster a sense of community that is unparalleled on this campus.

Our History

Four score and 7 years ago on a dark and stormy night eighteen men and women were huddled in a small, moist, dark room looking for answers. The very room in question is the space we now call our supply closet.

It was on this night that a bright idea was born, an idea so revolutionary that it has continued to grow daily since its inception.

 Although many thought this task seemed far too daunting and was seemingly impossible, we have risen against all odds and remained a strong force on campus. Many enemies continue to sabotage our establishment, but we have continued to remain victorious. Come join us in our valiant fight.
